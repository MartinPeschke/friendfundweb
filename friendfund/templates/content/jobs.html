Community/Marketing Manager (Berlin)

Friendfund is an innovative social commerce platform.

Job Description
You will be working in an international team in Berlin in which the business language is English.

You will be responsible for the following areas

  1. Content creation - writing blog posts, articles, newsletters.
  2. Social media– creating, managing and growing the company’s Twitter, Facebook.
  3. Public relations - managing incoming media requests and building relationships with industry journalists.
  4. Customer Support
  5. Marketing Strategy– Both offline and online. You will analyse and run facebook and google ad campaigns as well ensuring friendfund is covered in the press.

Your Experience
You should have an interest in crowd funding and social media in general. No specific experience is required but you are probably a person who has been involved in community based projects in the past.

Your Qualities
Intelligence.
Creativity.
Independence.
Ambition.
Fluent English and good German.

Our Offer
We offer good remuneration and the chance to work in a very dynamic and innovative team. You will be involved in the early stages of the company and have the chance to develop your responsibilities, skills and experience very rapidly.

Please send a cv to jobs@friendfund.com.

Frontend Developer/ JavaScript - AJAX – CSS (Berlin)

Friendfund is an innovative internet company which develops a social commerce platform and apps for networks such as facebook.

We believe in making all staff stake holders in the company and offer them the chance to develop and focus their energies in the areas in which they can express their talent and enthusiasm.

Job Description
You will be working in an international team in Berlin in which the business language is English.

This is a real opportunity to become an integral part of a creative and growing company. You will be responsible for feature implementation, product improvement, design changes and maintenance of friendfund.com and reporting directly to the founders.

Your development skills will range from smallish CSS tweaks to implementing fully redeployable javascript widgets. On the way you will pick up mobile skills through involvement in  the development of a HTML5/CSS3 mobile app. You will also have the opportunity to learn Python.

Your Experience
You should have an interest in (mobile) internet technology and social networking. Have prior work experience with at least one Javascript Framework (i.e. JQuery, Dojo, MooTools) and understand how their functionality can be implemented natively across different browsers. You bring expertise in JavaScript, XHTML/CSS, AJAX, JSON and other web standards and have at least one currently online web presence which showcases some of your work.

Your Qualities
Intelligence.
Creativity.
Independence.
Ambition.
Fluent English.

Our Offer
We offer a substantial share scheme, generous remuneration and the chance to work in a very dynamic, progressive and innovative team which is producing a ground breaking new type of social application. You will be involved in the early stages of the company and have the chance to develop your skills, responsibilities and experience very rapidly.

Please send a CV and cover letter to jobs@friendfund.com.
