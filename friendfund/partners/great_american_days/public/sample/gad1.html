Turn dreams of flying a plane into a reality during this 50 minute Introductory Hands-On Flight Lesson over Los Angeles County. During this exciting flight lesson, see what it is like to be a pilot flying a light aircraft, departing from either El Monte Airport, Brackett Field Airport, or Chino Airport in a Cessna 172. Prior to the flight lesson, you will receive a briefing from your Certified Flight Instructor on the techniques and safety aspects of light aircraft flying. After the briefing, climb into the Pilot's seat and prepare for takeoff. With the instructor there at all times, take over the controls after take-off and fly the plane. Enjoy the beautiful view of Southern California.

Cancellation/Rescheduling Policy
All changes to reservations for the flight instruction require 7 days notice.

How many people can be included on this lesson?
The flight instruction is for one participant. However, you may invite a guest to join you on the flight. Please note that the maximum combined weight of both passengers may not exceed 500 lbs.

Requirements/Restrictions
The maximum weight of the participant may not exceed 230 lbs. If you select to bring a guest, the combined weight for two participants must not exceed 500 lbs.
Participants must be at least 14 of age or older. Individuals who are pregnant, have broken bones or other physical conditions which are considered by the Instructor to be unsafe conditions are not eligible to participate. Cameras are welcome during the flight.

What should I wear for my flight?
Dress comfortably. Flat, rubber-soled athletic shoes are recommended.

Weather policies
The flight instruction may be canceled by the Pilot due to high winds, heavy rain, fog or other inclement weather. If flight is canceled by the Pilot, your flight will be rescheduled.

How long is the flying lesson experience?
The briefing before flight is 10 minutes, flight time is typically 50 minutes. Therefore, 1 hr total duration.

When can I schedule my flight?
The flight instruction is offered 7 days a week, year-round, subject to availability. Please make reservations as early as possible, we require a two weeks notice. Flights may be scheduled between 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM.
