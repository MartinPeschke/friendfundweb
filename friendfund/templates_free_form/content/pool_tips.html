Create a Pool for something tangible.

friendfund is about financial collaboration between friends for a specific project, whatever that may be. Your friends will love getting involved when the Pool is for something “real”.

Less is more.

Start by inviting those friends directly involved in your Pool. Less friends with genuine connection take ownership of getting the Pool funded. Too many friends with no connection leads to the thinking that someone else will do it!

Share!

friendfund has loads of great tools to share your Pool, including a widget for your site or blog.

Create a Pool with friendfund
