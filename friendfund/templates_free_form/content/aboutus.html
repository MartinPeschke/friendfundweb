About

Friendfund allows groups of friends to collect money. Whether for a group gift or helping out your local club, friendfund enables you to quickly invite a bunch of your Facebook, Twitter or email friends to chip in to a pool in a fun and social way.

friendfund it!
Our flexible social commerce platform friendfund.com can be used for just about anything that needs a simple way for a group of friends to collect money. With loads of great tools to spread the word about your pool reaching a contribution goal has never been easier (or as much fun).

Friendfund is also a great way to organise a group gift with our merchant integration. With the extensive catalogues of our partners, Amazon, Great American Days and Caseable, there is a gift for everyone!

Friendfund is simple to use. Create a pool. Invite your friends. Chip in!

We hope you enjoy giving together with friendfund.

Company

Friendfund was founded July 2010 with the aim of building a platform which synthesises the internet’s most progressive aspects, the social web and ecommerce.
Our goal is to provide the online community with the financial tools which harness the power of the social web in a fun and simple way.
Initially focusing on group gifting, the friendfund beta was released in October 2010 and with the invaluable feedback from our members, an upgraded site was released March 2011. Now the friendfund platform has opened up the group gifting model to allow groups of friends collect money for any nominated purpose.

Also improved is the sharing experience of a friendfund pool with widgets that can used on your website or blog and the creation of merchant white label integration with our partners amazon, Great American Days and caseable.
