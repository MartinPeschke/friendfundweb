The Basics

What is friendfund?Top

friendfund is a flexible social commerce platform that can be used for just about anything that needs a simple way for a group of friends to collect money.

Why have I received a friendfund pool invitation?Top

A friend has created a pool and invited you to chip in. Click on the Pools tab and you should see your friend's Pool in the “My Pools” section.

Creating a Pool

What is “All or Nothing”?Top

As the name suggests, for the Pool to be successful it needs to reach its target before the expiry date. Why? It is transparent: tell your friends what the Pool is for, they like the idea and contribute. Their contribution is only then used if the Pool gets enough support for that idea. It is also great motivation for the contributors to help you promote the Pool and make it happen!

Why is the limit set to 10 days?Top

Keeping it short and sweet, means you see the result faster. It also creates some urgency so you can keep the momentum of your Pool going.

What happens if we don’t raise enough money before the Pool closes?Top

friendfund pool credit card contributions are only debited when the Pool reaches its target and any contributions made with PayPal are refunded. There are lots of tools to send out reminders and share the Pool - so you shouldn’t have any problems drumming up the support for your Pool!

Can I get something for myself?Top

Yes. When using friendfund with one of our merchants simply select the recipient as yourself.

Inviting Friends

Why am I receiving messages from friendfund Pools?Top

friendfund is designed as a fun and social way for groups of friends to collect money. Pool messages help get everyone involved and excited about chipping and most importantly reaching the target! If you are not interested in receiving messages from the Pools you have been invited to, go to “Settings” in the drop down menu under your profile name (top right) and select the “Notifications” tab. You will find all the settings you need here.

I want my friends to opt into a Pool rather than being added through the invitation process.Top

Share the link with your friends and when they visit the Pool page they can click on the ‘Join Pool’ button.

Chipping in

Can I Chip In with another currency?Top

You can chip in to any pool, no matter the currency. Chip in with the currency stated on the pool and your Paypal or Credit Card provider will exchange your chip in amount to your local currency. More information about exchanges rates can be found here: PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, American Express

Can I get a refund or return the gift?Top

If, for any reason, the recipient is unhappy with their gift then the pool admin can return the product directly to the merchant and receive refund or an alternative product directly from them subject to the merchant's returns and refund policy.

What fees will I be charged?Top

As a contributor, you are not charged any transaction fees. When you create a Pool, you have the choice of receiving your funds as either a Merchant Voucher (free) or via PayPal.

Receiving payment via PayPal we charge a 1% fee to cover the transaction costs of your contributors. PayPal may also charge you a receiving fee. This amount varies depending on the type of PayPal account you have and where you are located. Please check with PayPal in your region for more details.

My Profile

How do I change my password?Top

Click your name at the top right of the screen. On the drop down menu select “Settings”, then select the “Password” tab.

Why do I have multiple accounts on the Profile Page?Top

friendfund allows you to combine your friend lists from different social networks. You can choose a name and match it with your picture from any of your network connections.

General

What is your Privacy Policy?Top

You can find all our policies in the “Legal” section of this site.

How do I delete a Pool?Top

Please email us support@friendfund.com, if you have already collected money we will be able to organise the refunds from our end.

How do I get my Pool to be a featured Pool?Top

The admin of a pool can email your Pool URL to our Community Manager: alice@friendfund.com. Let us know what makes your Pool great!

I have a suggestion for friendfund, who should I tell?Top

Great! You can leave a message in our Feedback Forum. Can’t wait to hear about your idea!
