What is friendfund?

1. What is friendfund?

friendfund is a flexible social commerce platform that can be used for just about anything that needs a simple way for a group of friends to collect money.

Top

2. Why have I received a friendfund pool invitation?

A friend has created a pool and invited you to chip in. Click on the pools tab and you should see your friend's pool in the “My Pools” section.

Top

Create a Pool

3. What is “All or Nothing”?

As the name suggests, for the pool to be successful it needs to reach its target within 10 days. Why? It is transparent: tell your friends what the pool is for, they like the idea and contribute. Their contribution is only then used if pool gets enough support for that idea. It is also great motivation for the contributors to help you promote the pool to make it happen!

Top

4. Why is the time limit set to 10 days?

Keeping it short and sweet, means you see the result faster. It also creates some urgency so you can keep the momentum of your Pool going.

Top

5. What happens if we don’t raise enough money before the Pool closes?

In this situation no Pool contributions are paid out. Payments made with Paypal are fully refunded and credit card contributions are not charged. There are lots of tools to send out reminders and share the pool - so you shouldn’t have any problems drumming up the support for your pool!

Top

6. Can I get something for myself?

Yes. When using friendfund with one of our merchants simply select the recipient as yourself.

Top

Invite Friends

7. Why am I receiving messages from friendfund Pools?

friendfund is designed as a fun and social way for groups of friends to collect money. Pool messages help get everyone involved and excited about chipping and most importantly reaching the target! If you are not interested in receiving messages from the Pools you have been invited to, simply go to “Account Settings” in the drop down menu under your name (top right) and select the “Notifications” tab. You will find all the settings you need here.

Top

8. I want my friends to opt into a pool rather than being added through the invitation process?

Simply share the link with your friends and when they visit the Pool page they can click on the ‘Join Pool’ button.

Top

Chip In

9. Can I Chip In with another currency?

You can chip in to any Pool, no matter the currency. Chip in with the currency stated on the Pool and your Paypal or Credit Card provider will exchange your chip in amount to your local currency. More information about exchanges rates can be found here:PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, American Express

Top

10. Can I get a refund or return the gift?

If, for any reason, the recipient is unhappy with their gift then the pool admin can return the product directly to the merchant and receive refund or an alternative product directly from them subject to the merchant's returns and refund policy.

Top

11. What fees will I be charged?

As a contributor you are not charged any transaction fees.

When you create a Pool you have the choice to receive the collected money as either a merchant voucher or via PayPal.

  • Merchant Vouchers do not have any fees.
  • Receiving payment via PayPal we charge a 3% fee to cover the transaction costs of your contributors. PayPal may also charge you a receiving fee, this amount varies depending on the type of PayPal account you have and where you are located. Please check with PayPal in your region for more details.

My Profile

12. How do I change my password?

Click your name at the top right of the screen, on the drop down menu select “Account Settings”, then select the “Change Password” tab.

Top

13. Why do I have multiple accounts on the profile page?

friendfund allows you to combine your friend lists from different social networks. You can choose a name and match it with your picture from any of your network connections.

Top

14. When connecting with my Facebook or Twitter account I receive a message saying that the account is already taken. Why is this?

This is because the particular network profile is already associated with another user. If you wish to use that account you must log out of friendfund and log in with that networks connect button and account.
