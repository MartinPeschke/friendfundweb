1. What is friendfund?

friendfund is a flexible social commerce platform that can be used for just about anything that needs a simple way for a group of friends to collect money.

Top

2. Why have I received a friendfund pool invitation?

A friend has created a pool and invited you to chip in. Click on the Pools tab and you should see your friend's Pool in the “My Pools” section.

Top

3. How do I change my password?

Click your name at the top right of the screen, on the drop down menu select “Account Settings”, then select the “Change Password” tab.

Top

4. What is “All or Nothing”?

As the name suggests, for the Pool to be successful it needs to reach its target before the expiry date. Why? It is transparent: tell your friends what the Pool is for, they like the idea and contribute. Their contribution is only then used if the Pool gets enough support for that idea. It is also great motivation for the contributors to help you promote the Pool and make it happen!

Top

5. What happens if we don’t raise enough money before the Pool closes?

friendfund pool credit card contributions are only debited when the Pool reaches its target and any contributions made with PayPal are refunded. There are lots of tools to send out reminders and share the Pool - so you shouldn’t have any problems drumming up the support for your Pool!

Top

6. Why do you have transaction costs?

This is to cover the transaction costs we have to pay to our credit card providers and PayPal, rates can be found in the Payment Method area on the "Chip In" page of your Pool.

Top

7. Can I get a refund or return the gift?

If, for any reason, the recipient is unhappy with their gift then the pool admin can return the product directly to the merchant and receive refund or an alternative product directly from them subject to the merchant's returns and refund policy.

Top

8. Can I get something for myself?

Yes. When using friendfund with one of our merchants simply select the recipient as yourself.

Top

9. What is your privacy policy?

You can find all our policies in the “About friendfund” section of the site.

Top

10. Why do I have multiple accounts on the profile page?

friendfund allows you to combine your friend lists from different social networks. You can set which network credentials you would like to use as your default in "Account Settings".

Top

11. I am connected with Facebook but see my Twitter profile picture and name.

This is because you have set Twitter as your default network on the "Account Settings" page. You can change this by clicking on your name at the top right corner of the screen and selecting “Account Settings” from the drop down menu and set Facebook to be your default profile.

Top

12. When connecting with my Facebook or Twitter account I receive a message saying that the account is already taken. Why is this?

This is because the particular network profile is already associated with another user. If you wish to use that account you must log out of friendfund and log in with that networks connect button and account.

Top

13. How can I change the currency of my Pool?

When you are creating your pool, on the Create Pool page, you can roll over and click on the currency symbol. A scroll down menu will appear with the choice of CA$, Fr., €, £, $.

Top

14. Can I Chip In with another currency?

You can chip in to any pool, no matter the currency. Chip in with the currency stated on the pool and your Paypal or Credit Card provider will exchange your chip in amount to your local currency. More information about exchanges rates can be found here:PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, American Express

Top

15. How do I delete a Pool?

Please email us support@friendfund.com, if you have already collected money we will be able to organise the refunds from our end.

Top

16. How do I get my Pool to be a featured Pool?

The admin of a pool can email your Pool URL to our Community Manager: alice@friendfund.com. Let us know what makes your Pool great!

Top

17. I have a suggestion for friendfund, who should I tell?

Great! Click on the “Feedback” tab on the left side of the screen. It will take you to our Feedback Forum. Can’t wait to hear about your idea!

Top

18. I have read all the FAQ’s and I still have a question. Now what?

Check out out Feedback Forum: the feedback tab on the left side of the screen. Still got a question? Create a new topic in the forum or you can email us at support@friendfund.com.
