How it works

1. Whilst viewing any product page on the merchant site, customers can choose to “friendfund it” by clicking on our button. This takes them to a white-label popup where they can start organizing their group gift.

2. In the popup, customers can choose their “recipient” and occasion.

3. Invite facebook, twitter or email friends to contribute, give the Pool a name, and add a personal message.

4. Pool creation.

The Pool page is a dynamic social environment where friends chip in and leave personal messages.

When each Pool reaches its funding target, friendfund does the rest.
