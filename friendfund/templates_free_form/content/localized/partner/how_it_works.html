How it works

1. Whilst viewing any product page on the merchant site, customers can choose to “friendfund it” by clicking on our button. This takes them to a white-label popup where they can start organizing their group gift..

2. In the popup, the user chooses which friend they would like to get the gift for and when the special occasion is.

3. Now the user invites their Facebook and twitter friends to contribute to gift.

4. Invited friends visit the pool page where they can view the gift, see who else has contributed and chip in themselves.
